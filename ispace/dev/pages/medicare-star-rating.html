PAYER – Medicare Star Rating

Medicare Advantage Health Plans improve their star ratings and revenue reimbursements through prospective data analytics and workflows that help proactively design and deliver intervention programs to address care gaps and other quality focused outreach programs.

Care Metrics is a comprehensive, end-to-end data analytics and interventions platform that helps Health Plans and providers improve their CMS star ratings by delivering actionable insights and enabling design and execution of member outreach programs.

Features

  • Comprehensive end-to-end data analytics and interventions platform
  • Data Integration
  • Data Analytics
  • Outreach management and collaboration

Benefits

  • Analyze your current ratings and scores across all domains and measures
  • Identify root causes and opportunities for improvement of gaps in care
  • Prioritize projects with highest return
  • Track and review progress of interventions
