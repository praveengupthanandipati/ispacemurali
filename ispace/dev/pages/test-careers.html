iSpace, Inc. (El Segundo, CA) needs IT Project Manager:


Manages assigned IT projects to ensure adherence to budget, schedule, and scope of project. Prepare the Project estimates and Project Management Plan that consists of Project Scope/Objectives, Infrastructure/Staffing requirements, Commitments made to Customer, Customer Responsibilities, Project Schedule, Work Break down structure (WBS) and technologies, based on the information available in the PIN and Contract/SOW. Conduct project kick-off meeting with relevant stake holders. Coordinate with all the project stakeholders for the successful completion of the project. Schedule and facilitates the Project meetings with the required Project stakeholders throughout the execution of the project. Helps the Customer in identifying their needs and priorities by understanding their business. Research and evaluate hardware and software technology options and weigh the cost/benefit analysis when making large purchases on behalf of the company. Communicate with IT Infra for infrastructure requirement and Project folder creation and its access to the team in configuration management tool. Identify the resource requirement at the start of the Project and during the execution of the project for supplemental resources. Hires and trains members of the technical staff. Assign roles and responsibilities to the Project personnel. Perform risk assessments and prepare Risk Management Plan and revise this during the review of Project risks. Monitor and tracks the project progress by conducting periodic project progress reviews w.r.t the Project schedule and deliverables. Conducts regular Team Review meetings to Review the status of tasks assigned to team members, to know the challenges and identify the causes for the delays (if any) and to identify the Issues that need management attention. Provides technical guidance to the team members of the Project and helps the team when required. Prepares a weekly project status report on the status of the project and submits to management and client. Review and revise the Project Estimates and Project Management Plan, Project Schedules, Risk Management Plan when there are major changes to requirements or major deviation in effort or schedule. Timely updates to Project Management Plan, Project Scheduled tasks/Work Tracker and Project data collection worksheet (Schedule, effort and defects) as project progresses. Reviews the progress of project with the customer as per contractual terms and commitments made to the customer and reviews Issues that need to be resolved by the customer Planning and implementing the necessary steps in case Project is not meeting the Target value of Quality Objectives Track and Review the Budget to make sure that Project is in adherence to planned budget. Ensures quality of deliverables and submits project deliverables to the client. Conducts Project Closure Meeting with Client and shares MOM. Conducts project closure meeting with Project Team, SQA, HR and IT Infrastructure Support Team. Prepares the Project Case Study and works on Project Cost Vs Profit Validation. Monitor and document the Performance of the Project team members and provide the feedback to the team after Project Closure or during Performance Appraisals. Travel/relocation required as jobs will be performed at unanticipated locations throughout the United States.
