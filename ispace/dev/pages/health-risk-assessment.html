PAYER – Health Risk Assessment

Health Risk Assessment solution for visiting nurses and physicians to engage and assess patients with complex conditions and needs. The solution provides workflow for queue handling and intelligent routing to optimize productivity.

Features

  • Single sign on
  • Secure access and data encryption
  • Local storage, auto sync and on demand sync
  • HRA templates and assessment questionnaires configurable by plan and program
  • Appointment scheduling and nurse routing
  • Drug data integration
  • Auto-save and carry forwards

Benefits

  • Elimination of paper based HRAs
  • Efficient routing and management of appointments
  • Improved cycle time for submissions of HRAs
  • Rich analytics from transactional data and coded forms
