Geo Tracking

 

Our GeoTracking Solutions are designed for highly scalable appliations (50 TPS, Low battery usage). Our solutions include server side and client side GeoFencing, light weight embedded client agents and cloud based server API’s. Other features include custom notifications and complete location history and reporting.

Location of Things

 

Our solution offers precise, seamless indoor/outdoor location determination across a wide variety of devices and applications utilizing TCS’s IoT Location Platform (ILP). ILP platform provides multi-sensor hybrid positioning and assistance data services by tracking GPS position through Cellular and Wi-Fi platform.

iSpace Fleet and Driver Management Platform

  • Real time Tracking – Instantly track all vehicles in real-time. Create GeoFence boundaries to alert when vehicles stray off route
  • Route Management – Optimize daily routes based on traffic, construction and real-time customer needs
  • Driver Behaviour – Monitor and track driver behavior- speed, acceleration, braking, idle time etc.
  • Vehicle Health – Track complete vehicle telematics information to monitor fleet performance, schedule planned and unplanned maintenance, end-of-life vehicles.

Usage Based Insurance

 

Insurance companies would be able to compute premium based on driving patterns (derived from Driving Score), a more data driven approach. Drivers would be more cautious to get better pricing on their premiums
